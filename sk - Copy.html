One Full Day Tour Plan

Start your day with a heritage walk in the heart of Kolkata. Explore the colonial-era architecture, visit St. John’s Church, Writer’s Building, and the General Post Office. Don’t miss the iconic Dalhousie Square and immerse yourself in the historical charm. Visit the magnificent Victoria Memorial, an architectural marvel dedicated to Queen Victoria. Explore the lush green Maidan (Esplanade) nearby. Enjoy a leisurely stroll, take in the serene ambiance, and capture beautiful photographs.
