Two Days Tour Plan

Start your day with a heritage walk in the heart of Kolkata. Explore the colonial-era architecture, visit St. John’s Church, Writer’s Building, and the General Post Office. Don’t miss the iconic Dalhousie Square and immerse yourself in the historical charm. Visit the magnificent Victoria Memorial, an architectural marvel dedicated to Queen Victoria. Explore the lush green Maidan (Esplanade) nearby. Enjoy a leisurely stroll, take in the serene ambiance, and capture beautiful photographs. Along with that in this package you will also be able to explore parts of North Kolkata such as Jorasanko Thakur Bari(Tagore's ancestral home), St. John's Church and Marble Palace.

Three Days Tour Plan

In this tour package you will be able to explore all the notable places across both North and South Kolkata starting from colonial structures such as the Victoria Memorial, to places like Jorasanko Thakur Bari and College Street. You can also pay your respects at the Kalighat Temple, one of Kolkata’s oldest and most revered temples. Then, head to the Dakshineswar Kali Temple on the banks of the Hooghly River. The temple’s stunning architecture and spiritual aura are captivating. Explore the bustling New Market area. Indulge in a food trail—try local delicacies like kathi rolls, phuchka (pani puri), and sweets from iconic sweet shops. Don’t forget to sip on a refreshing glass of shikanji (lemonade). This package is profitable for those people who have sufficient time in their hand and will be able to explore Kolkata to it's fullest.
