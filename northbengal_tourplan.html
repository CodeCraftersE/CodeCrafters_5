Classic Darjeeling Tour

• Duration: 4 days/3 nights
Highlights:
• Visit Tiger Hill for a breathtaking sunrise view over the Himalayas.
• Explore the famous Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park.
• Enjoy a ride on the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway (Toy Train).
• Visit the Peace Pagoda and Batasia Loop.
• Leisure time at the Mall Road for shopping and local cuisine.

Tea Garden Experience

• Duration: 5 days/4 nights
Highlights:
• Guided tour of renowned tea estates like Happy Valley Tea Estate.
• Learn about the tea-making process and enjoy tea tasting sessions.
• Visit the Himalayan Mountaineering Institute and the Everest Museum.
• Explore the beautiful landscapes around Darjeeling.

Adventure and Nature Tour


• Duration: 6 days/5 nights
Highlights:
• Trekking to Sandakphu, the highest peak in West Bengal.
• River rafting on the Teesta River.
• Visit the Singalila National Park for wildlife enthusiasts.
• Explore the picturesque landscapes of Mirik and its lake.

Honeymoon Special


• Duration: 8 days/7 nights
Highlights:
• Romantic sunrise view at Tiger Hill.
• Private candlelight dinner overlooking the Himalayas.
• Visit the beautiful Senchal Lake for a romantic boat ride.
• Stay in cozy, romantic accommodations with scenic views.
