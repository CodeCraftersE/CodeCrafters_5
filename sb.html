Two Day, One Night Tour Plan

Day 1:
Start early from Kolkata and reach Godkhali, the gateway to Sundarbans. Take a boat cruise through the picturesque mangrove forests. Check in to your eco-resort or forest lodge. Enjoy a thrilling boat safari spotting wildlife like Royal Bengal tigers, crocodiles, and birds.
Spend the night at your accommodation, enjoying a traditional Bengali dinner.

Three Days, Two Nights Tour Plan

Day 1:
• Take a morning train or bus from Howrah to Canning, the gateway to the Sundarbans.
• From Canning, hire a private boat to take you to your resort in the Sundarbans. The boat ride is a great way to see the mangroves up close and spot some wildlife, such as crocodiles and dolp
• Check in to your resort and relax for a bit. In the afternoon, head out on a jeep safari to explore the forest. Keep your eyes peeled for deer, wild boars, and even tigers!
• In the evening, enjoy a delicious dinner at your resort and then settle in for a night of stargazing under the clear sky.
Day 2:
• In the morning, take a boat ride through the narrow waterways of the Sundarbans. This is a great way to see the unique flora and fauna of the forest, such as the Royal Bengal Tiger.
• In the afternoon, visit the Sajnekhali Watchtower, which offers stunning views of the Sundarbans.
• In the evening, enjoy a cultural program at your resort, followed by dinner.

Three Days, Two Nights Tour Plan

Day 1
: Take a morning train from Howrah to Canning station. From Canning, hire a car to reach the Sudhanyakhola jetty. Take a motorboat from Sudhanyakhola jetty to Dobanki, the gateway to the Sundarban National Park. Check in to your forest rest house. In the afternoon, take a boat safari through the narrow creeks of the Sundarban. Spot wildlife like crocodiles, deer, and monkeys. In the evening, enjoy a cultural program performed by the local villagers.
Day 2
: In the morning, take a boat safari to Sajnekhali Watchtower, one of the best places to spot the Royal Bengal Tiger. In the afternoon, visit the Sudhachari Crocodile Project, where you can learn about the conservation of crocodiles. In the evening, enjoy a bonfire and a night safari.
Day 3
: In the morning, take a boat safari to Bhagabatpur Crocodile Project. In the afternoon, visit the Netidhopani Watchtower, another good place to spot the Royal Bengal Tiger. In the evening, enjoy a farewell dinner and a cultural program.
Day 4
: In the morning, check out of your forest rest house and take a boat safari to Sudhanyakhola jetty. From Sudhanyakhola jetty, take a car to Canning station. Take a train from Canning station back to Howrah.
