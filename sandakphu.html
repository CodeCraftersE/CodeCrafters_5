Sandakphu Trekking Expedition

• Duration: 7 days/6 nights
Highlights:
• Trek to Sandakphu, the highest peak in West Bengal.
• Enjoy breathtaking views of Mt. Everest, Kanchenjunga, Lhotse, and Makalu.
• Explore the picturesque landscapes of Singalila National Park.
• Overnight stays at trekking lodges or campsites

Adventure and Wildlife Safari

• Duration: 8 days/7 nights Highlights:
• Trek to Sandakphu for the mountain views.
• Safari in Singalila National Park to spot diverse flora and fauna.
• Explore the charming village of Mane Bhanjang.
• Visit the nearby Chitrey Monastery.

Cultural Trekking Experience:


• Duration: 6 days/5 nights
Highlights:
• Trekking to Sandakphu, the highest peak in West Bengal.
• Visit the remote villages along the trekking route.
• Attend cultural events and interact with local communities.
• Experience the warm hospitality of the local people.

Photography Expedition


• Duration: 5 days/4 nights
Highlights:
• Capture stunning landscapes and mountain vistas during the trek.
• Early morning photography sessions at Sandakphu for sunrise views.
• Explore the flora and fauna of Singalila National Park.
• Photography mentorship for enthusiasts
