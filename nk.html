One Full Day Tour Plan

If you don't have a lot of time in your hand then this short tour plan is perfect for you. It is a one day plan in which you will be able to explore the historic streets of North Kolkata, visit colonial era buildings, and learn about their architectural significance. Other stops will also include Jorasanko Thakur Bari(Tagore's ancestral home), St. John's Church and Marble Palace. Through this you will be able to discover the rich cultural heritage of Bengal through art, literature and music. Not only that but you will also be able to embark on a gastronomic adventure! You will be able to visit iconic eateries in College Street and Burrabazar.

Two Days Tour Plan

This two days tour plan will cover the whole of North Kolkata. You will be able toexplore the historic streets of North Kolkata, visit colonial era buildings, and learn about their architectural significance. Other stops will also include Jorasanko Thakur Bari(Tagore's ancestral home), St. John's Church and Marble Palace. You will also be able to embark on a gastronomic adventure! You will be able to visit iconic eateries in College Street and Burrabazar. Along with that you will be able to explore Kumartuli, which is the artisan quarter where skilled potters create exquisite clay idols for festivals. You will be able to witness the craftsmanship and learn about Durga Puja traditions.
