Heritage Tour Package


• Duration: 2 Days, 1 Night
• Accommodation: Stay at a heritage hotel or guesthouse in Bishnupur.
• Activities: Visit terracotta temples such as the Jor Bangla Temple, Rasmancha, Madan Mohan Temple, Lalji Temple, explore the Bishnupur Museum, witness the art of terracotta pottery-making, attend a local cultural performance (if available).
• Meals: Breakfast included.
• Transportation: Private or shared transport from Kolkata or nearby cities to Bishnupur and back.

Temple Tour Package


• Duration: 1 Day
• Accommodation: Day trip from nearby cities like Kolkata or Asansol.
• Activities: Visit major temples in Bishnupur including Rasmancha, Jor Bangla Temple, Madan Mohan Temple, Lalji Temple, and others, explore the intricate terracotta art and architecture, learn about the historical significance of each temple.
• Meals: Lunch included (local cuisine).
• Transportation: Private or shared transport from Kolkata or nearby cities to Bishnupur and back.

Nature and Heritage Tour Package


• Duration: 3 Days, 2 Nights
• Accommodation: Stay at a heritage hotel or eco-resort near Bishnupur.
• Activities: Visit terracotta temples and historical sites in Bishnupur, explore nearby natural attractions such as Joypur Forest, Susunia Hills, Mukutmanipur Dam, participate in nature walks, birdwatching, and outdoor activities.
• Meals: Breakfast included.
• Transportation: Private or shared transport from Kolkata or nearby cities to Bishnupur and back
