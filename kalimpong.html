Kalimpong Delight

• Duration: 3 days/2 nights
Highlights:
• Visit the Durpin Monastery for panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.
• Explore the flower-filled nurseries and the Deolo Hill.
• Stroll through the bustling market of Kalimpong.
• Visit the Zang Dhok Palri Phodang Monastery.

Nature and Adventure Tour

• Duration: 5 days/4 nights
Highlights:
• Explore the Neora Valley National Park for its rich biodiversity.
• Enjoy river rafting in the Teesta River.
• Visit the Lepcha Museum and understand the local culture.
• Trek to the nearby offbeat trails for a unique experience.

Kalimpong and Sikkim Combo


• Duration: 7 days/6 nights
Highlights:
• Explore both Kalimpong and nearby attractions like Pelling in Sikkim.
• Visit the Pemayangtse Monastery and Rabdentse Ruins.
• Enjoy a cable car ride in Gangtok.
• Explore the scenic beauty of Yuksom in Sikkim.

Cultural Immersion in Kalimpong


• Duration: 6 days/5 nights
Highlights:
• Attend local festivals and cultural events.
• Visit the Lepcha Museum and understand the indigenous culture.
• Explore the Dr. Graham's Home, a missionary school with a rich history.
• Spend time in the local markets for a taste of authentic local life.
